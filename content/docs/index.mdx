The <Brand /> payment gateway lets you securely integrate payment processing into
your application using an **Access Key** and **Secret Key**. This enables seamless
online payments, beneficiary management, fiat balances and OTC FX directly from
your platform.

To access these APIs you must have an **active access key and secret key**, issued
through the <DashboardLink>merchant dashboard</DashboardLink>.

## Environments

<Brand /> exposes two independent environments. They share the same API surface —
point your client at sandbox while building, switch to production when you go live.
